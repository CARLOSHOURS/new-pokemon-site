The Legend of the Oresia Continent

▌Mysteries of the Dimensional Rifts

Every century, glowing dimensional rifts appear across the Oresia continent. These anomalies connect to parallel worlds, each opening unleashing entirely new species of Pokémon...

"The cries echoing from the rifts were both familiar and foreign... definitely not from any Pokémon we know."

▌The Seven Biomes of Oresia

The continent is divided by mysterious energy into seven distinct ecological zones, each home to unique Pokémon species:

  • Crystal Tundra: Ice Pokémon evolved under shimmering auroras
  • Quantum Jungle: Flora bears stat-enhancing berries
  • Ruined Mechopolis: Nest of steel and electric Pokémon
